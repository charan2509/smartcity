About SmartCity

Smart City is a web-based Application which stores the details of a city. The main purpose of this project to help the Students, Job seekers and other visitors to the city by providing information about hotels, transportation facilities , Tourist places, Educational Institutions, job openings, etc. The Smart City project serves the visitors as a guide of the city


Services provided by SmartCity,



Jobs


Education




Hotels


Tourism





Job Vacancies


You can find various job openings and access the information about the job offerings present in the city this service helps you to find jobs more quickly and efficiently




Education


You can find all the schools and universities present in the city this makes it easy for students to get better access to the information about the educational Institutions




Hotels and Resturants


You can use this service to find various hotels and restaurants present in the city this can help you if you are person who is stranger to the city, By using this serice you can find the hotel and restaurants which are near to your locations




Tourism


You can find the popular locations which are popular in the city using this service, This can help you to find the best destination for your taste and can help you to enjoy your holiday time in a better way
